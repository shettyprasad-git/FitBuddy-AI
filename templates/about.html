FitBuddy AI

Your Intelligent Fitness Companion

FitBuddy AI is a production-ready AI-powered fitness and wellness platform that brings personalised coaching to everyone — regardless of fitness level, budget, or location.

Built with Python Flask, Bootstrap 5, and Chart.js, the application integrates seamlessly with IBM watsonx Orchestrate to deliver intelligent, context-aware fitness guidance through a natural conversation interface.
